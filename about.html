About the Diaspora Technical Working Group

The Diaspora Technical Working Group is a dedicated coalition of Kenyan citizens residing in countries in the Africa region , Asia+Oceania region, Europe region, North America region, South America region and Middle East region, united by a shared commitment to enhancing the voting rights of the Kenyan diaspora.

The team is composed of diverse professionals from various sectors, including law, technology, education, business, healthcare, and research. Their collective expertise is vital for developing and implementing effective strategies that facilitate diaspora voting.

Membership within the group is drawn from influential leaders across professionals, political, religious, business, and diaspora associations/organizations. The team reflects Kenya's rich diversity, including representatives from multiple ethnicities, youth, women, and individuals with disabilities, ensuring that its operations are inclusive and representative of the entire Kenyan diaspora. `
